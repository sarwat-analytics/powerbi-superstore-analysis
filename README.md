# Superstore Sales & Profit Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview
This Power BI project analyzes Superstore sales, profit, customer segments, product performance, and regional loss patterns using an interactive multi-page dashboard.

The project was designed to simulate a real-world business intelligence reporting environment where stakeholders can monitor KPIs, identify loss-making products, evaluate regional performance, and explore sales trends through interactive visualizations.

---

## Business Objective
The goal of this project is to transform raw retail sales data into actionable business insights through interactive reporting and analytics.

The dashboard helps answer important business questions such as:

- Which regions generate the highest sales and profit?
- Which products contribute most to losses?
- How do sales and profits vary over time?
- Which customer segments perform best?
- What are the top-performing and low-performing categories and sub-categories?

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Sales Performance Overview
Provides a high-level business summary including:
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin %
- Year-over-Year Sales Growth
- Quarterly and Monthly Sales Trend comparison
- Sales by Product Category
- Sub-category level KPI summary table

### 2. Product Performance & Profitability Analysis
Focuses on detailed product and sub-category analysis:
- Product Sub-Category Sales Performance
- Product Sub-Category Profitability
- Sales vs Profit comparison by sub-category
- Top 10 Products by Sales and Profitability
- Top Product Sales KPI
- Top Product Profit KPI
- Lowest Product Profit KPI

### 3. Geographic Sales & Profit Analysis
Analyzes regional and geographic business performance:
- Sales vs Profit by Region
- Regional Contribution to Total Sales (%)
- Regional Contribution to Total Profit (%)
- Sales Distribution by Customer Segment
- Geographic Sales Distribution by State
- Top and Lowest Performing Regions

### 4. Profitability Risk Analysis
Highlights business loss and risk areas:
- Loss-making product analysis table
- Loss Contribution by Region
- Loss Contribution by Customer Segment
- Total Loss KPI
- Highest Loss Region identification
- Highest Loss Product identification
- Region and category-based profitability risk filtering

## Key Insights
- High sales do not always result in high profit.
- Certain products consistently contribute to business losses.
- Regional performance varies significantly across categories.
- Office Supplies and Furniture categories require closer profitability monitoring.
- Interactive filtering allows deeper exploration of trends and performance patterns.

---

## Data Modeling
The project uses a structured star schema data model to improve reporting performance and analytical flexibility.

Key modeling components include:
- Fact and dimension table structure
- Dedicated Calendar/Date table
- Optimized relationships between tables
- DAX measures for KPI calculations
- Time intelligence analysis using Date functions

The data model was designed to support scalable reporting, interactive filtering, and efficient dashboard performance.
